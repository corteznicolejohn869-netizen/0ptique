OPTIQUE Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [Insert Date Here]

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide the Service, process your orders, and enhance your shopping experience.

a. Personal Information (Data You Provide)

  • **Account Data:** Name, Email Address, and Password (hashed) used for **login** and **register**.
  • **Order Data:** Shipping Address (for Checkout), payment information (we do not store full credit card information), and phone number.
  • **Communication:** Any information you provide to us through the **Contact** form or email.

b. Non-Personal Information (Automatically Collected Data)

  • **Shopping Experience:** Items added to **Cart** and **Wishlist**, products viewed, and search queries.
  • **Device Information:** IP address, browser type, and operating system.
  • **Local Storage/Cookies:** Used to save the status of your **Cart** and **Wishlist** and your login status (`USER_STATUS_KEY`).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • **For the Service:** To process and fulfill your orders, and provide customer service.
  • **For Account Management:** To manage your account on **myaccount.html** and track your login status.
  • **Personalization:** To offer product recommendations based on your browsing history and **Wishlist** content.
  • **Marketing:** For sending newsletters (if you subscribe via the footer form) or promotional updates.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your Personal Information. We only share it with:

  • **Third-Party Service Providers:** Companies that provide shipping, payment processing, or marketing services. They are only authorized to use your data for these services.
  • **Legal Requirements:** If required by law or in response to valid legal process.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect your personal information, but no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We ask that you protect your account by using a strong password.

5. Your Rights

  • You can update or delete your account information by logging into your account (**myaccount.html**).
  • You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link.

For questions regarding this policy, please contact us.
